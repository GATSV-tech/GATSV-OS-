— 01  What It Is

The OS that
runs your
business.

GATSV OS is not an automation tool. It's a full operating layer — a system that screens inbound work, routes it to the right agent, and keeps you informed without flooding your inbox.

Every action is logged. Every agent is auditable. You stay in control through a single approval interface in Slack — no dashboards to check, no emails to triage, no dropped balls.

Built for founder-led service businesses and small agencies. The kind of operation where you're the bottleneck — and we remove that bottleneck without removing you from the loop.

— 03  The Flow

Five steps.
Zero dropped balls.

01
Request Arrives
Via iMessage, email, or Slack. Doesn't matter where — it all goes through the same pipe.
02
Gatekeeper Screens
Classifies intent, checks for duplicates, scores priority. No LLM call wasted.
03
Router Assigns
Decides which operator handles it. Sales, support, delivery, or founder review.
04
You Approve
One message in Slack. Approve, reject, or defer. The OS waits for your signal.
05
Operator Executes
Action taken, logged, and confirmed. Full audit trail, no surprises.
